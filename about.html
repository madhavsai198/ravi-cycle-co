Our Story

Three decades of pedaling joy, fitness, and freedom to families across India

From a Small Shop to India's Trusted Cycle Brand

Founded in 1995 by Ravi Kumar in the heart of Bengaluru's Gandhi Bazaar, Ravi Cycle Company started as a humble repair shop with a big dream — to make quality cycling accessible to every Indian family.

Over the past three decades, we've grown from a single shop to 5 showrooms across Bengaluru, serving over 50,000 happy riders. Our passion for cycling drives us to curate the best bicycles for every age, terrain, and budget.

Today, we offer a wide range of mountain, road, hybrid, kids, and electric bicycles — all backed by our legendary after-sales service and cycling expertise.
